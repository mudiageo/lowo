# Lowo Final Demo Script 🎬

This script is designed for a **2-4 minute** walkthrough of the Lowo Budget Planner.

## Phase 1: Onboarding (0:00 - 0:45)

- **Start**: Launch the app from a clean state.
- **Narrative**: "Welcome to Lowo, your local-first personal finance manager."
- **Action**: Quickly move through Steps 1-3 (Name, Income, First Budget).
- **Security**: In Step 4, enable **PIN Protection**. Demonstrate setting a 4-digit PIN.
- **Launch**: Click "Let's Go!" to land on the Dashboard.

## Phase 2: Budgeting & Tracking (0:45 - 2:00)

- **Dashboard Overview**: "Our dashboard gives you an instant Oklch-powered view of your status."
- **Category Polish**: Go to **Budgets**. Show the categories.
- **NEW**: Edit a category (e.g., Change "Food" allocation). "We can now tune our budget live."
- **Adding Expenses**: Use the horizontal FAB to log a quick expense. Show how it updates the progress bar instantly.

## Phase 3: AI & Security (2:00 - 3:00)

- **AI Insights**: Go to **Insights**. Generate an analysis. "Lowo runs Google Gemini directly on your device for absolute privacy."
- **Security Guard**: Lock the screen (or reload).
- **Keyboard Entry**: "No need for touch-only; we can use our physical keyboard to unlock." Type the PIN.
- **Biometric**: Mention WebAuthn support for Face ID/Fingerprint.

## Phase 4: PWA (3:00+)

- **Conclusion**: "Lowo is a fully offline-ready PWA. No app store, no servers, just your data and your control."
